**Understand any text instantly.** Paste anything — emails, essays, books, tweets. Get word counts, emotion DNA, frequency maps and a live word galaxy. No fluff. Just insight.

## 🎯 What is LEXIS?

LEXIS is a powerful yet elegant text analysis tool that decodes language with precision. Whether you're a writer polishing prose, a student analyzing essays, a copywriter crafting persuasive content, or an analyst reviewing documents, LEXIS reveals the hidden patterns and emotional undertones in your text.

### ✦ Key Features

LEXIS provides **four powerful lenses** to understand any text:

#### 1. **Word & Letter Count** ◈
- Total word count, letter count, and unique vocabulary analysis
- Animated precision counters
- Perfect for writers tracking length and density

#### 2. **Emotional DNA Scanner** 🫀
- Live EKG waveform visualization
- Detects 6 core emotions: joy, anger, sadness, fear, surprise, and disgust
- Watch your text's emotional mood shift in real time
- Real-time emotion tracking as you type

#### 3. **Word Constellation** ✦
- Every unique word becomes a drifting star in an interactive galaxy
- Brightness indicates word frequency
- Shared words create constellation connections
- Hover over words to explore relationships

#### 4. **Frequency Maps** ▤
- Visual representation of word frequency distribution
- Identify key themes and focal points
- Understand vocabulary patterns at a glance
